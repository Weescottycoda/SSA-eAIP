# Tower

The responsibility of Tower at Diass falls to the dedicated Tower ATS Unit, Diass Tower (GOBD_TWR) on 118.800. TWR will be responisble for the movements on the runway, as well as the responsibility of ensuring safety amongst VFR aircraft operating in the circuit or within the Diass CTR from GND - 3000ft MSL

## Visual Flight Rules (VFR) Aircraft

| Type | Circuit Altitude |
| :---------: | :---------: |
| Jet | 1300ft |
| Turbine / Piston | 1300ft |
| Helicopter | 1300ft |

 - All circuits are conducted left hand noramally, but may be conducted right hand at the discrection of the Tower controller.

!!! info "Circuit Clearence"
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, Left hand circuits, not above altitude 2000ft, report L/R downwind Ruway XX.

!!! info "Circuit Clearence (Non STD)"
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, non standard Right hand circuits, not above altitude 2000ft, report L/R downwind Runway XX.

### VRP Map

!!! info VRP Map
    There are no Visual Reference Points at GOBD.

### VFR Departures

There are no standard VFR routings for departure out of GOBD.

!!! info
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, cleared to exit the N'Djili CTR to the N / S / E / W, not above altitude 2000ft report leaving the CTR / report "VRP".

### VFR Arrivals

There are no standard VFR routings for arrivals into GOBD.

!!! info
    All standard VFR routes will require pilots to be given further instructions to join the circuit. None of the standard routings favour a certain runway.

## Takeoff Phraseology

!!! info "Takeoff (Full Length)"
    ZSABC, Runway XX, wind 080 degrees at 9 knots, cleared for takeoff, report airborne.

!!! info "Takeoff (Intersection)"
    ZSABC, Runway XX at XX, wind 080 degrees at 9 knots, cleared for takeoff, report airborne.

!!! info "Handoff"
    ZSABC, Contact Diass Approach 120.500.
