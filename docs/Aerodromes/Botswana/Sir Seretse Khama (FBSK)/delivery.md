# Delivery 

!!! info 
    Sir Seretse Khama does not have a dedicated Delivery frequency, as such all delivery responsibilities are assumned by Khama Ground (FBSK_GND). Under no circumstances should a FBSK_DEL position ever be opened without prior approval from staff.
