# Delivery

!!! info
    Wonderboom does not have a dedicated Delivery frequency, as such all Delivery responsibilities are assumed by Wonderboom Ground(FAWB_GND). Under no circumstances should a FAWB_DEL position ever be opened without prior approval from staff.
