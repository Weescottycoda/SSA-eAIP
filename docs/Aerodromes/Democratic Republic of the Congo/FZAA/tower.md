# Tower

The responsibility of Tower at N'Djili falls to the dedicated Tower ATS unit, N'Djili Tower (FCBB_TWR) on 118.100. TWR will be responsible for the movements on the runway, as well as the responsibility of ensuring safety amongst VFR aircraft operating in the circuit or within the N'Djili CTR from GND - 2000ft MSL.

## Visual Flight Rules (VFR) Aircraft

| Type | Circuit Altitude |
| :---------: | :---------: |
| Jet | 2000ft |
| Turbine / Piston | 2000ft |
| Helicopter | 2000ft |

 - All circuits are conducted left hand normally, but may be conducted right hand at the discretion of the Tower controller.

!!! info "Circuit Clearence"
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, Left hand circuits, not above altitude 2000ft, report L/R downwind Ruway XX.

!!! info "Circuit Clearence (Non STD)"
    ZSABC, hold position, after departure Runway XX, non standard Right hand circuits, not above altitude 2000ft, report L/R downwind Runway XX.

## Takeoff Phraseology

!!! info "Takeoff (Full Length)"
    ZSABC, Runway XX, wind 080 degrees at 9 knots, cleared for takeoff, report airborne.

!!! info "Takeoff (Intersection)"
    ZSABC, Runway XX at XX, wind 080 degrees at 9 knots, cleared for takeoff, report airborne.

!!! info "Handoff"
    ZSABC, Contact N'Djili Approach 119.700.
